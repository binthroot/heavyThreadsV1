Our Work

Backpacks & Outdoors

This durable and lightweight hiking pack is built with premium materials to keep your gear secure and comfortable on trail. We stock multiple colors and deniers of Ultra, X-Pac, ECPACK and HDPE Gridstop fabrics for the body of the pack as well as UHMWPE ripstop stretch mesh & net mesh for the pockets. For more details please contact us to design your next pack.

Pouches & Straps

Buckle Straps, Velcro Straps, shoulder straps, and zippered pouches of all sizes. Just let us know the size you need and we can meet your specifications.

Embroidery

Branding your products with professional embroidery tells your customers that you mean business. Just send us your art file and we will get you a quote.

Custom Services

This is where we shine. If you are in need of American made custom sewn goods, please contact us with the details of your project. We specialize in Berry Compliant Custom Bags, Pouches and Strap Assemblies. We design, pattern, prototype, and manufacture all in one shop.
